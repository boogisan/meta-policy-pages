Data Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your data collected by our app, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to yourappsupport@email.com (replace with your support email).
  2. Include your full name and the email address associated with your Facebook account.
  3. Use the subject line: “Data Deletion Request”.

Once we receive your request, we will process it within 7 business days and delete all user-related data from our servers.

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact us.
